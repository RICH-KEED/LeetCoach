LeetCoach Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Overview

LeetCoach is a Chrome Extension that analyzes LeetCode submissions using AI. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

We do NOT collect or store any of your data on our servers.

The only data the extension processes is:

  • LeetCode page content — Problem title, description, constraints, and your submitted code. This is extracted locally in your browser.
  • Your AI provider API key — Stored securely in your browser's Chrome sync storage. We never see or transmit this key to our servers.
  • Extension settings — Provider preferences, model name, temperature, max tokens. Stored locally in Chrome storage.

How Data Is Used

When you submit a solution on LeetCode:

  1. The extension extracts submission data locally in your browser.
  2. The data is sent directly from your browser to your chosen AI provider (OpenAI, Gemini, Groq, OpenRouter, or custom endpoint).
  3. The AI response is received directly in your browser and displayed in the sidebar.

At no point does your code or problem data pass through LeetCoach servers.

Third-Party Services

The extension communicates with AI providers you configure. Please review their privacy policies:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • OpenRouter Privacy Policy

Cookies and Tracking

LeetCoach does not use cookies, trackers, or analytics.

Your Rights

You can uninstall the extension at any time to remove all stored data. You can also clear Chrome storage via your browser settings.

Contact
